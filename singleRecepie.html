Pizza Margarita

Preparation Steps

Dought

  1. Put all the flours and salt in a mixing bowl.
  2. In another mixing ball of a smaller size, stir together 260 grams lukewarm tap water, then add the olive oil and the yeast, and pour all of that in the flour mixture. Then proceeded to knead with your hands until it is well mixed, approximately 4 minutes, then let the mixture rest for 20 minutes.
  3. Cut the dough in 2 equal pieces and shape each into a ball. Place on a heavily floured surface, cover with a dampened cloth, and let it rest and rise for 4 hours at a room temperature.
  4. Finally place each dough ball on a heavily floured surface and use your fingers to stretch it, then using your hands to shape it into rounds.

Pizza

  1. Preheat the oven to 500 tiers for 30 mins.
  2. Now, unfold the pizza sauce over the pizza dough. Then sprinkle Parmesan cheese over the dough, accompanied via way of means of mozzarella cheese and pepperoni slices.
  3. Place paper towels on a microwave-secure plate, evenly spread the mozzarella cheese and spinach at the plate, and sprinkle with cover with paper towels to prepare dinner for 30 seconds. Use the paper towels to sop up any greater grease and set aside.
  4. Finally, use the pizza peel to slip the pizza onto the preheated pizza stone. Bake for 8-12 mins. Let the pizza take a seat down for five mins earlier than cutting and serving.
  5. Repeat with the ultimate dough and toppings.

Ingridients

  • 3 cups warm water
  • 1 ½ teaspoon sugar
  • 3 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 8 cups all-purpose flour, plus more for dusting
  • 5 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil, plus more for greasing
  • 1 teaspoons kosher salt
  • ¼ cup semolina flour
  • 1 cup of mozzarella cheese
  • 15 leaves of spinach
